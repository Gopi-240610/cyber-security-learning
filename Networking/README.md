## What is Networking?
Computer networking is the practice of connecting computers and devices to share data and resources.
## Types of Networks
-LAN(Local Area Network)
-MAN(Metropolitan Area Network)
-WAN(Wide Area Network)
## Network Devices 
-Router
-Switch
-Hub
-Modem
-Firewall
## OSI Model(7 Layers)

7.Application
## Protocols
-TCP/IP
-HTTP/HTTPS
-FTP
-DNS
## Why Networking is important in Cyber Security?
-Helps understand attacks
-Used in monitoring traffic
-Protects data transmission
